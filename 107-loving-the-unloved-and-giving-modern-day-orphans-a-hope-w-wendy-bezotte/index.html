107: Loving the Unloved and Giving Modern-Day Orphans a Hope w. Wendy Bezotte

Dan

The question of the day is..... If you don't like the way things are, what are you doing to change them?

We all say that we want to make the world a better place, feed the homeless, love the unloved, but are we really taking action to do it?

Today's episode is my conversation with Pastor Wendy Bezotte. Wendy has a passion for foster children and wants to help them have a better and more fulfilling life, so against all odds she started a local camp for abused and neglected foster children to attend each year. It's a place where they can experience the childhood that they never had and that the rest of us take for granted. It's also a place where they can learn and understand that they are truly loved, even though the world has repeatedly told them otherwise.

Are you willing to sacrifice just a little bit of yourself to potentially save another human being? Because the opportunity is there and it's a whole lot easier and more impactful than you might think.

All that and more on today's episode of The System is Down, Episode 107: Loving the Unloved and Giving Modern-Day Orphans a Hope w. Wendy Bezotte!
