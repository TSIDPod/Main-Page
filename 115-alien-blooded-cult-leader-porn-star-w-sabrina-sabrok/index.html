115: Alien Blooded, Cult Leader, Porn Star? w. Sabrina Sabrok

Dan

The question of the day is..... What team are you on? What tribe are you in? What cult are you a part of?

While that may seem like a strange QOTD to lead today's episode, I think it's quite fitting.

I've been noticing tribes EVERYWHERE lately. You find people who agree with your worldview, you click, and eventually you start looking at "outsiders" as the enemy. Personally, I don't buy it and I think that's why our numbers for this show are a rollercoaster.

People see "Nephilim" in the title of last week's episode, they peak in to see what it's all about and assume that this show is a Christian Gen 6 conspiriacy show... then the next week I pull the rug out by interviewing a former porn actress, black magic cult leader who claims to have alien DNA.

I'm not about tribes. I'm about open-mindedly exploring the world and civilly humbly listening to people's stories, whether I agree with their worldview or not. I believe the world needs open-minded conversation FAR more than it needs more tribes. So, if you have a problem with me talking to a "cult leader", check yourself and leave your own cults before jumping to conclusions.

That said, today's episode is my conversation with Sabrina Sabrok. Sabrina is a former adult film actress, a musician, a TV personality, a witch, a cult-leader, and she claims that she has alien DNA and speaks with inter-dimensional beings, so she's a perfect candidate to share her story on this weird show.

Enjoy!
