165: Mother's Day Special w. Mom & My Siblings, Jessica & #FireCraig

Dan

Happy belated Mother’s Day!

If you’ve been listening to this show for any length of time over a year, then you are familiar with our annual Mother’s Day special.

For the past 2 years, the Mother’s Day special has been me interviewing my mom one on one. This year, we decided to up the ante by having my two older siblings join the conversation as well.

So that’s what today’s episode is… My mom, Jesica, #FireCraig, and I having a casual, civil and often “uncomfortable” lengthy conversation on all the topics… politics, Christianity, Vermin Supreme, nostalgia, libertarianism, POTUS, and much much more.
