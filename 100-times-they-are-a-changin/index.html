The question of the day is........ What the hell do you know?

Well, ladies and gentlemen, the time has come.... the big 100. When I started the show, I never really imagined it would make it this far, but here we are. Countless hours, guests, and uncomfortableness under our belts and still going strong.

In this, the grand 100 episode of the show, I decided to switch up a few things around here. We need to keep things fresh and progressing and I just don't think the good clean look and feel of yesteryear fits this show's mission well enough anymore.

We are the punk rock of podcasts. We're daring to be raw and real in an age and industry that is all too often glossed up beyond recognition. It's time to be bold, humble, and honest, if we're truly going to change the world, one uncomfortable conversation at a time.

There's no guest this week because I've just got too much shit on my mind that I need to get out there. So that's what this episode is, just me spewing my thoughts about the state of things along with listening to some happy voicemails and making some big announcements.

The most important of these announcements is that I'm launching a project to begin the first TSID documentary called The Moon The Myth The Legend: Lunar Lore for Humble Dummies. I really want to kick things up a notch and challenge people now and in the future to humbly open their mind and explore the world.

Why do some people still believe the moon landing was a hoax? Why does everyone else seem to think the hoaxers are human garbage? Is it because there's zero evidence pointing towards that conclusion? Spoiler alert: No. Duh.

All that and oh so much more on today's episode of The System is Down episode 100: Times They Are A-Changin'

For more information on and to preorder The Moon The Myth The Legend, visit http://moonmythlegend.com
