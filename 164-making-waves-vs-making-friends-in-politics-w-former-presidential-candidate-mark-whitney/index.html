Happy hump day! Enjoy a bonus midweek episode on us.

Today's episode is our conversation with former presidential candidate for the Libertarian Party, Mark Whitney. This episode was recorded in advance and unfortunately, Mark suspended his campaign before I got around to releasing the conversation. Had I released it before then, perhaps he'd still be with us. Who knows? I do. That's false.

Anyway, regardless of whether he's still in the race, I thought it was an enjoyable discussion with a lot of good insight and information from Mark, so I decided to still give it to you. You're welcome.

During his campaign, Mark came to be known as the brash candidate who wasn't afraid to step on some toes and use some "potty words" to get your attention. As a former felon and comedian, he brought a lot of fun and color to the debate stage as he pushed strongly with his message heavy on criminal justice reform.

Was his crassness and toe-stepping all an act? Is Mark Whitney just a big ol' asshole? And my oh my, what did such a polite young man do to end up in federal prison?
