101: How to Fake an American Moon Landing w. Massimo Mazzucco

Dan

The question of the day is.... How much do you really know about the moon landings?... Not the hoax theories, but just the official story.

We all go thru our day to day lives assuming that we know things, but do we really? It's easy to say that men have walked on the moon and anyone who thinks otherwise must be crazy, but what if the "crazy" person actually knows a heck of a lot more than you do on the topic? How can you call someone crazy for coming to a conclusion on a topic that you know nothing about?

Today's episode is my conversation with the great documentarian, Massimo Mazzucco. Massimo digs into all sorts of controversial topics and does an amazing job at presenting his research and findings in a way that everyone with an open mind can understand.

The topic of his most recent doc is the theory that the moon landings were a hoax and that man has, in fact, never walked on the moon. You can jump to conclusions and say it's crazy, but what are you basing that belief on? Do you know anything about the moon landing? Have you done your research?... or are you just happily accepting what you're told, without any foundation to back it up?

Is there any evidence to prove that the moon landing was fake? Is there any evidence to prove that the moon landing was real? What the hell does Stanley Kubrick have to do with any of this?

Find out now on The System is Down, episode 101: How to Fake an American Moon Landing w. Massimo Mazzuco
