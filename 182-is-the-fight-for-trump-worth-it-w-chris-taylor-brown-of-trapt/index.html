The question of the day is… Is it worth the risk to stand up for your beliefs, even if it jeopardizes your business?

To a younger generation, perhaps you heard of Trapt or Chris Taylor Brown from memes and online controversies around social media political feuds. I learned about Trapt in a very different way... as a 14 year old kid... when I sneakily listened to the rock radio stations that my parents forbid. Now, 18 years later (almost to the date), what a weird world we live in.

Today's episode is my conversation with a childhood inspiration of mine, Chris Taylor Brown of the band Trapt. It was a great fun civil chat about Chris' history, how he became a diehard Donald Trump supporter, things that he regrets from his online feuds and so much more.

Will Chris sway me to the Trump side? Find out now on today's episode of The System is Down episode 182: Is the Fight for Trump Worth It? w. Chris Taylor Brown of TRAPT.
