You know... There are just not enough hours in the day... It's something that I heard adults say many times when I was a child and teen, but there are seriously not enough hours in the day. Between eight hours of shutting your body down to 8-10 hours of work to family, friends, hobbies, etc,... life is just so full these days... And I'm not saying this to complain. I have a great life, a great job, a great family, and great hobbies, all of which I greatly enjoy, but there's just not nearly enough time to do all the things I want to accomplish in this life...

Anyway, that's totally unrelated to the content of this episode, but the reason why this episode is coming out a couple hours later than usual.

Today's episode is my conversation with repeat offender, Remso Martinez. Remso just wrote and released his first book, Stay Away from the Libertarians. This is not just an hour long plug for the book. I actually have not even yet read it, but my copy should be coming in the mail today and, after our discussion, I'm pretty stoked to jump into it. In this episode we dug into a lot of topics.. updates on the plethora of things that Remso has been up to, what happened to his show, Remso Republic, where he's taken his ghost hunting endeavors, and a whole bunch of his personal and political experiences and ideas. I might even get him to elude to his unspoken plans in running for office.

Why should you "Stay Away from the Libertarians"? What does this book bring new to the table of discussion? Why is Remso so annoyed by Anarchists? All of that and more on today's episode of The System is Down!

Let's get weird!
