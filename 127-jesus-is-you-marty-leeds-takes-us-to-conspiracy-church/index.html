127: “Jesus is You”? Marty Leeds Takes Us to Conspiracy Church

Dan

The question of the day is.... Who is Jesus?

Apparently around this time every year, I start to get that controversial itch that only Marty Leeds can scratch.

Marty has been a friend of the show since close to the beginning and he's always one of my favorite people to have on as he's not afraid to seek out ultimate truth and share in, regardless of how controversial his findings may be.

For this, his third visit, Marty and I dig into more of his spiritual beliefs. Marty's views and interpretations on Jesus, christianity, the bible, and virtually all things spiritual are very different than what you learned in Sunday school and if he's right, we've all got a lot of re-thinking to do.

Is Marty a heretic, a shill, an antisemite, a flat-tard? Is there any hope for western christianity and humanity at large? All of that and more on today's episode of The System is Down #127: "Jesus is You"? Marty Leeds Takes Us to Conspiracy Church
