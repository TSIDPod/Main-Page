19: A Democrat, A Libertarian, and A Republican Talk and No-One Gets Triggered

Dan

Today's episode was an extremely fun one. I got a Democrat, a Libertarian, and a Republican together to discuss hot button political and social issues and somehow... everyone was civil and respectful to each other. Go figure...

The conversation went longer than it was intended to, but far shorter than I would have like, so this is the first of another two-parter, the second of which will be coming out next Monday. My awesome guests were, Ryan Beckenbaugh, a lawyer and ardent Bernie Sanders Supporter, Marc Clair, the host of the Libertarian-centric Lions of Liberty Podcast, and Jon Schweppe, a republican writer forThe National Pulse.

So, a democrat, a libertarian, and a republican walk into a bar... Sounds like the start of a joke right? Enjoy.
