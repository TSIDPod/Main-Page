If you've been paying any attention, it should come as no surprise that I'm a fan and supporter of Vermin Supreme in his presidential run this year. He's a joke candidate, but if you take the time to listen to him speak, you'll soon realize he's oh so much more. Vermin is in fact, a very intelligent, caring, and principled libertarian. In my opinion, he's a win win. You get a strong message for liberty, wrapped in a crazy attention getting middle finger to the beyond broken political system. What's not to love.

Today's episode is our conversation with Vermin's campaign manager, Desarae Lindsey. The whole idea of putting Vermin up as a serious candidate for the first time in his 30+ years of joke campaigns, was her's. Desarae was also a large part of John McAfee's campaign in 2016, so she clearly has an affinity for helping oddballs make some noise and she does it well.

Is there more to Vermin Supreme than meets the eye? Is Desarae a glutton for political punishment? And how do you run a serious joke campaign?
