QAnon Chronicles 00: Knock, knock, Neo.

Dan

Welcome to the latest addition to The System is Down podcast, QAnon Chronicles.

If you dabble at all in conspiracies, watch the nightly news, or even just browse the internet, you've likely heard buzz about the QAnon theories.

We did a main episode on Q way back in August of 2018 with my personal friend, Scott McElroy, who had dug far deeper into the theory than I. A lot has unfolded since that time and I personally wanted to know more and keep up to date without having to constantly scour the net looking for reliable sources and code breakers.

So, I've decided to add this show to the TSID roster to help keep everyone informed of the more serious and weird happenings that seem to be unfolding that the nightly news doesn't seem to find important enough to report on.

There are many who say that Q is 100% truth and Donald Trump will be the savior of mankind. There are many who say Q is a bunch of conspiracy nonsense and shouldn't even be looked at. We're not in either of those camps. Whether it's true or not, it should be investigated, talked about, and documented as a part of our history.

If it all turns out to be bunk, then fine. In that case, it's a fascinating look at a bizarre insanely elaborate prank that happened. However, if there's more to it and even a shred of truth, then it's infinitely more important than that and I don't know about you, but if there are crazy revelations being exposed from behind the curtain, I don't want to be the one who turned a blind eye and assumed it was nonsense without taking a look.

So, as we do here on The System is Down, let's peak behind the curtains of socially acceptable comfortable status-quo society and go boldly forward together to try to figure out what is and what isn't.
