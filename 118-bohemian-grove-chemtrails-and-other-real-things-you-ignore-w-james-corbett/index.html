118: Bohemian Grove, Chemtrails, and Other Real Things You Ignore w. James Corbett

Dan

The question of the day is.... How strange does something have to be before you stop ignoring it.

Most people, when they hear about things like Bohemian Grove and Chemtrails, if they even know what they are, they quickly jump to the assumption that these are things made up by some crazy conspiracy theorist youtubers in their parents' basement.

Now these are two of many "conspiracy theories" that are, in fact (not opinion) 100% proven to be true, but still, despite their bizarre and unsettling nature, the vast majority of people choose to ignore them... Why?

Today's episode is my conversation with the great James Corbett of The Corbett Report. We dug into some of the history of these topics, the things that people should know about them, and the implications that they have, which people seem willingly ignorant to.

James is a journalist who has been respectfully and rationally working to expose uncomfortable topics for over a decade does some of the best work in his field.
